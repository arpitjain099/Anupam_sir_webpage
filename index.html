Vision

Compliant and Robotic Systems Laboratory

My research intent is to comprehend diverse designs in nature that are time-tested and robust,and implement assimilated concepts for optimal form designs in engineering problems. I solve design problems through innovative/inverse design in Compliant and Robotic Systems(CARS). Industrial applications are predominant in product design, precision instrumentation,sensing/actuation, MEMS/NEMS, humanoid robotics, automated highway systems, exploration, surveillance, transportation, and in prosthetic, orthotic and exo-skeletal devices.


Interests: Design of compliant mechanisms and MEMS, Structural Topology Design/Optimization,Computer Aided Engineering Design and Graphics, Geometrically/Materially Large displacement Finite Element Analysis, Robotics/Kinematics.
